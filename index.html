Who We Are

The Church of Christ welcomes you.

The Euclid Church of Christ is a congregation of Christians striving to serve God according to the pattern of the New Testament. We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and our only guide in religious matters. No human creed, no denominational ties — our goal is to simply be Christians, nothing more and nothing less.

Our worship follows the simple, meaningful pattern of the New Testament: we pray, sing, partake of the Lord's Supper, give as we have been prospered, and hear the Word of God proclaimed.

Every first day of the week we meet at 635 East 250th St, Euclid, OH for worship and fellowship in spirit and in truth.
